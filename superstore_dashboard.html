06 · Strategic Insights

Recommendations

Actionable findings drawn from the data above.

Opportunity

Double Down on Technology

Technology generates 36% of revenue at a 17.4% margin — the highest of any category. Phones ($330K) and Copiers ($150K) are both high-volume and high-margin. Skew sales resources, marketing spend, and B2B outreach toward Technology, particularly high-ticket hardware bundles aimed at Corporate and Home Office segments.

Critical Risk

Fix or Exit Tables & Bookcases

Tables generated $207K in revenue yet lost $17,726 — a −8.6% margin. Bookcases similarly lost money. These products consume sales effort and warehouse cost while destroying value. Recommended: raise list prices 12–18%, remove promotional discounts on these SKUs, or discontinue low-velocity items.

Warning

Repair the Central Region

Central delivers $501K in revenue but only $39.7K in profit — a 7.9% margin versus a 12.5% national average. That 4.5-point gap represents roughly $22.5K in "missing" annual profit. A regional audit should check discounting behavior, product mix, and sales rep practices.

Action

Implement a Strict Discount Policy

Discounting correlates negatively with profit (−0.22): every extra point of discount erodes margin. Furniture discounts in particular push already-thin margins into loss territory. Recommended: require approval for discounts above 15%, and eliminate discounting on any sub-category under a 10% margin.

Action

Capitalize on Seasonal Q4 Peaks

November is consistently the highest-revenue month every year, with November 2017 hitting a single-month record of $118K. Plan inventory, temporary staffing, and promotions no later than September to capture the Q4 surge without margin compression from rush sourcing.

Growth

Develop the South Region

South is the smallest region at $392K revenue but holds an 11.9% margin — comparable to East. Demand quality is there; volume is underdeveloped. Targeted expansion via additional headcount or distribution could realistically add $100K–$150K in annual revenue at healthy margins.
